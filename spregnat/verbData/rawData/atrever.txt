atreverse
REFLEXIVE VERB
1. (to have the courage) 
a. to dare 
Los niños no se atreven a meterse al agua fría.The children don't dare go in the cold water.
2. (to be insolent) 
a. to dare 
No te atrevas a hablar durante el examen.Don't you dare speak during the test.
